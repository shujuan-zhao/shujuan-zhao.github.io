## Design Philosophy

The redesign follows academic website best practices:
1. **Clear professional identity** - Immediate understanding of research focus
2. **Impact-driven content** - Leading with achievements and contributions
3. **Easy navigation** - Logical tab structure for different content types
4. **Comprehensive profiles** - All major academic platforms linked
5. **Clean aesthetics** - Maintained Contrast theme's minimalist design

## Technical Implementation
